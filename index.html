Instructor FAQs

How do I create a course?

To create a course, log in to your instructor account, navigate to the 'My Courses' section, and click 'Create New Course'. Fill in the course details and upload your content.

How can I track student progress?

Once your course is live, you can view detailed analytics in the 'Course Management' section. This shows student engagement, completion rates, and more.

How do I set course pricing?

You can set a price for your course while creating it or by editing it in the 'My Courses' section. There are multiple pricing options available, including subscription and one-time fees.

Can I add quizzes or assignments to my course?

Yes! You can create quizzes and assignments under the 'Course Management' section. You have full control over the type of assessments and the grading system.

How do I interact with students?

You can interact with your students through discussion forums, live Q&A sessions, and email. We also provide a messaging system within the platform.

Can I offer discounts for my course?

Yes, you can create discount codes for your courses. The discount can be applied during checkout by students.

Student FAQs

How do I enroll in a course?

Browse our course catalog, select the course you're interested in, and click 'Enroll'. For free courses, you'll be enrolled instantly, while paid courses require payment first.

Can I watch courses on mobile?

Yes! Our platform is fully mobile-optimized, so you can watch courses through our app or any mobile browser. Courses are accessible anywhere!

How do I track my course progress?

You can track your progress on the course page, where you'll see your completion percentage and which lessons have been completed.

Can I get a certificate after completing the course?

Yes! After completing a course, you can receive a certificate of completion. The certificate is available for download from your course dashboard.

General FAQs

How can I contact support?

You can contact support through the 'Contact Us' section or by submitting a support ticket in the 'Submit a Ticket' section at the bottom of the page.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page, click on 'Forgot Password', and follow the instructions to receive a reset link in your email.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods. For subscription-based courses, payments are automatically renewed monthly or yearly.
